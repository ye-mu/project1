Top 3 in 2005-2020

Fullmetal Alchemist

Fantastic and self-consistent story. Ranked 1st as “the best Japanese animation” in most Western ACG websites. I watched all the 64 episodes in three days.

BACCANO!

Really interesting story telling - 5 stories in different periods and locations, yet deeply connected, are “randomly” showed. I yelled out when I finally understand the whole story.

Little Witch Academia

Just relax and see these lovely characters having their adventures. You could find something familiar – the story reminds me of Disney and Harry Potter.
